01 — What we do

In plain terms

Three pillars cover most of what clients hire us for. You can buy them separately, combine them in a bundle, or use the free call to decide what to prioritize first.

Web presence

Multi-page sites, landing pages, and focused pages for offers or ads—structured so visitors know what you sell and how to reach you.

Social & discovery

Profile setup, bios, launch posts, and light content planning so you look active and credible where people search and scroll.

Brand consistency

Logo direction, colors, and type choices that match your site and social so everything feels like one business.

02 — Service detail

Browse what we deliver

Use the arrows to move through each area. Each slide lists typical deliverables; your proposal may vary slightly after we confirm scope.

Websites & pages

Multi-page sites for services, menus, portfolios, and contact—built mobile-first and easy to skim.

  • Home, services, about, contact (or your custom structure)
  • Clear calls to action: call, form, or booking link
  • Basic on-page structure for search (titles, headings, local cues)
  • Optional: extra pages (pricing, FAQs, areas served)

Landing pages & campaigns

Single-page builds when you need one strong message—ads, promos, waitlists, or seasonal offers.

  • One primary goal per page (lead, call, or signup)
  • Copy structure and section layout tuned to the offer
  • Fast turnaround when scope is locked
  • Can match creative from an existing ad or brand sheet

Social profile setup

We configure profiles so a stranger understands your business in seconds—not after ten scrolls.

  • Bio, highlights, and handle alignment where possible
  • Profile and cover visuals sized for the platform
  • Starter posts so the feed is not empty on day one
  • Second platform available as an add-on

Brand & visual system

Enough rules to stay consistent without a 40-page PDF nobody reads.

  • Logo mark or refresh direction (deliverables agreed in scope)
  • Color palette and font pairing for web + social
  • Simple templates or examples for posts and stories
  • Guidelines export when included in your package

Content planning

Calendars and prompts so you are not staring at a blank screen every week.

  • 1-week or 4-week calendars with topics and cadence
  • Caption angles and hashtag suggestions per slot
  • Optional: short-form video or Reel script outlines
  • Aligned with offers you want to push that month

Local & extras

Smaller items that support everything above—often added à la carte or folded into larger bundles.

  • Google Business Profile copy and category alignment
  • Link-in-bio style single scroll page
  • Email capture block and export-friendly setup
  • Light SEO checklist and meta basics for new sites
